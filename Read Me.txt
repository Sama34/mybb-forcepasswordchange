Force Password Change 1.1
MyBB 1.6 Plugin

Author: Will Pillar
Website: http://willpillar.com



Description
-------------------------------
This plugin allows admins to force a single user to change their password or force one or more usergroups to change their passwords. 


Installation
-------------------------------
To install Force Password Change, you need to do the following:

1. Upload admin/modules/user/forcepwchange.php to admin/modules/user/ on your server.
2. Upload inc/plugins/forcepwchange.php to inc/plugins/ on your server.
3. Upload inc/languages/english/admin/forcepwchange_admin.lang.php to inc/languages/english/admin on your server.
4. Upload inc/languages/english/forcepwchange.lang.php to inc/languages/english/ on your server.
5. Goto the ACP>Configuration>Plugins and click Install & Activate.
6. The ACP menu for the plugin will appear in the sidebar in ACP>Users&Groups.

If you have any problems you can contact me on the MyBB Community Forums under the username 'Nitrus'.
